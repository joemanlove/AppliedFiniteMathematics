\section{Reduction by Dominance Puzzles}

Reduce the payoff matrix by dominance. Find the optimal strategy for each player and the value of the game.

    \[
        \begin{bmatrix}
            2  & -1 \\
            0  & 3  \\
            -2 & 0
        \end{bmatrix}
    \]
\end{puzzle}

\begin{puzzle}

\end{puzzle}
